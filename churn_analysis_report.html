04Findings & Response
Key Findings
  • Tuned Logistic Regression performs best, reaching AUC 0.862
  • Top factors driving churn: Contract, OnlineSecurity, tenure, InternetService, TotalCharges
  • Customers with short tenure and high monthly charges are at higher risk
Retention Strategy
  1. Offer discounts or incentives for month-to-month customers to switch to longer contracts
  2. Provide stronger tech support and online security bundles for high-risk segments
  3. Prioritize retention outreach on customers ranked in the top decile of churn probability
