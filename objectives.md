# Overview
- Predict customer churn for a telecom company. This project demonstrates how to identify customers at risk of leaving the service, enabling proactive retention strategies.

# Objectives
- Identify customers likely to leave the service, 
- understand key factors driving churn, 
- and develop targeted retention strategies to improve customer lifetime value.

# Methodology
- Logistic Regression modeling
- Decision Trees classification
- Feature engineering & selection
- Model evaluation & hyperparameter tuning
